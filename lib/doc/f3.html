Preface

Fudger is a tool for helping table-top roleplaying gamemasters keep track of their campaigns. It is primarily designed for Fudge, and as such is not really intended as a "numbercruncher", but rather an idea organizer.

Fudger is written in Ruby and requires ruby-gtk libzip-ruby ruby-gtkmozembed and ruby-gettext in order to run.
